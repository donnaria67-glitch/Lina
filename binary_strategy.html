Binary Crypto Trading Strategy

Key Factors for Binary Trading

  1. Asset selection:
    • BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC
  2. Time frame: Short, Medium, Long-term
  3. Price direction: Call, Put, Neutral
  4. Strike price: Near current market
  5. Expiry time: Align with analysis
  6. Payout percentage: 70%-90%
  7. Risk management: Budget, limit positions, stop-loss
  8. Market analysis: Technical & Fundamental
  9. Broker selection: Reputable
  10. Regulations: Compliance
  11. Leverage: Caution
  12. Trading strategy: Consistency & discipline
